Find The Number!!

How to Play:


  1. Start the Game: Click the "Start" button to begin.

  2. Reveal the Target Number: The "Hit" button will display the number you need to find.

  3. Search for the Number: Identify and click on the correct number hidden among the bubbles.

  4. Score Points: Your score will increase with every correct selection.

  5. Time Limit: The game ends when the timer runs out.
