Tapping Order

How to Play

  1. Click the "Start Game" button to begin.

  2. Observe the tiles light up in a specific sequence. Remember the order carefully!

  3. Click on the tiles in the exact order as shown. Each round adds one more tile to the sequence.

  4. The game gets progressively harder as the sequence grows longer.

  5. If you click the wrong tile, the game ends. Try again to beat your high score!
