Game Rules

  1. Start the Game:
    • Choose the duration of the game from a dropdown menu.
    • Once the game starts, the timer begins counting down.
    • When the timer reaches zero, the game ends automatically, and the final score is displayed.

  2. Paddle Control:
    • The paddle moves horizontally at the bottom of the screen.
    • Control the paddle using the left and right arrow keys on the keyboard.
    • The player must move the paddle to catch the falling ball.
    • When the ball touches the paddle, it bounces back upward, and the player earns a point.

  3. Scoring:
    • Each time the ball hits the paddle, the player earns one point.
    • The score is displayed at the top of the screen and updates with every successful catch.
    • The player must catch as many balls as possible within the chosen time.
